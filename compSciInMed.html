Healthcare and the contribution of Computer Science

Healthcare is REALLY important! It does stuff like

  • Keeping people alive
  • Stopping disease
  • Ambulances

Computer science has an underrated contribution to this field with some examples shown below

Research
Research is at the forefront of medical advancements - high performance computing can simulate complex biological processes to aid in medical understanding and research
Analysis
Computational tools aid in more accurate analysis of medical scans - AI is playing an increasing role in this field
Electronic Health Records
Storing health records online using technologies such as databases can make it easier for hospitals to share data on patients and remove loss of data
